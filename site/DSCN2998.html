1087
Dan head. Dan or wudan. Black fringed bangs appear under wind cap feng mao 風帽. Pink ground with black, greenish blue, red, yellow cut decos. Blue band ties cap at nape. Elaborate front ornament with four small pink to red pompoms and one large surround cut emblem at center. Pink profile flower with green leaf deco at ear; small pale lavender ear ring. Pale flesh face, pink blush from brow to cheek.
1088
Dan head. Dan or or wu dan. Black hair with twin green deco ivory meifa 美髮 sticks in figure eight at back, bound with red, yellow clasp. Blue to pale twisted cloth forms right angle deco at hair. Large pink, blue, green flower deco at ear connects to blue band with cut red, green yellow front emblem topped by large red pompom and black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Black filigree cut extensions at nape. Small green ear ring. White face with pale rouge. Small red lips.
