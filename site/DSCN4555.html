1780
Shen gui head, demon. Shen gui head. Old female Demon. Flesh face, cut wrinkles, red closed lips. Natural filigree cut hair bound up. Small lotus crown with black pompom and natural ruyi-like emblem; earring.
1781
Sheng head. Sheng. Black open cut face. Black hair fringe below diadem, ezi 額子. 4 large red pompoms; red tassel at ear. Black hair knob at back above black back cloth.
1782
Dan head. Dan. Black open cut face; small earring. Black hair bound up in yellow twisted ribbons. Large natural pompom over central front emblem. Long yellow ribbon hangs below chin; black filigree back cloth.
