Shen gui head, bird. Bird king head. 3/4 black face, yellow bindi. Yellowish pointed and curved beak with smiling teeth, lips at edge. Round black eyes look up. Tufted cut brows, black ear tuft and pate. Small black crown, cut red, green yellow decos. 1 large red pompom.
