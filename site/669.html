669

type
figures
description
Four Guards or Porters. Walking side-by-side carrying upright poles, 1 halberd and toggle, 2 3-pointed halberd, 3 open hand, 4 hand clenching a brush pen, all with real bamboo staves sewn to fisted hands and along length of staves. Seen ¾ view, second one in profile, pockmarked face, all expressive beardless chou faces; red porter hats with top knot jewel and black brim; long queues visible at back; red, front button green stiff collar, wide sleeve tunics over black close sleeve under garment; dark green cuffs, trousers tuck into black cut boots. 2 rods.
dimensions
H: 16 x 13 ½; figure 11 x 3 ¼; rods 6, 9.
