Jing head. Jing. Marquis. Hou mao 候帽 or er bu wen 耳不聞 hat. Large black pompom above front emblem; in red, green, ochre, black. Ochre hexagonal filigree pattern on side panel of helmet. black painted face, white above eye; white filigree opera style beard covers mouth; ochre neck. No lacquer.
