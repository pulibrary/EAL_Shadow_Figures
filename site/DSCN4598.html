1901
Chou head. Chou. Soldier. Military. Flesh face, square open cut clown patch; lips, teeth, tongue. Helmet of ¾ tiger face and mane. Red to orange cut brows above round crossed eyes, red mouth. Cut whiskers, ears, black, grey, ochre stripes down back. Large green pompom. Lacquered.
1902
Chou head. Chou. Flesh face, cut wrinkles near nose, fish shape eye, closed red lips. Green circlet. Black jaguar-like animal helmet, spots are cut out, ¾ view animal head, red to orange cut brows above round crossed eyes, red mouth. Cut whiskers, ears, repeat grey mane.
1903
Chou head. Chou. Black jaguar-like animal hat, cut out spots, ¾ view animal head, similar to tiger. Flesh face, cut wrinkles near nose, triangle eye, eye patch.
