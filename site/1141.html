chou_head
description
Chou head. Chou. Blue zhanmao 氈帽 felt hat with wide brim and fishtail crown perched on top of head; brim slightly pulled down. Black hair bound loosely at nape with red cord. Pink painted face with stylized clown patch; stylized black tufted brow and stachios to sideburns. Grey mark on cheek, red mark above brow, black marks on patch. Red lips.
dimensions
H: 4 ¼ x 3.
