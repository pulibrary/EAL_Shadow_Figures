2123
Military commander female. Wu dan, commanding general warrior body found with attached green faced head, see 2124. Highly ornate blue wide sleeve armor-dress with cut flower decorations and repeat edge of peacock feather ends showing eye; matches 4 vertical flags at back. Tippet; wide sleeves over close sleeves. Jade belt clenched in jaws of upward facing profile green face. In blue, green red, black. Block hands; lotus feet. 3 rods.
2124
Dan head. Dan, green face, red brows and hair; closed lips. 2 meifa 美髮 stick at back hold '8' shape hair; large flower decorations at ear. Tufted bangs and brow; straight filigree cut extends back below neck. Unique.
