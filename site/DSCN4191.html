1672
Shen gui head, dragon. Head of dragon or demon. Profile face, two round crossed eyes; open mouth, teeth; yellow whiskers curling in different directions. Red jagged beard, ear tufts, bushy brows. Small crown, 1 large red pompom.
1673
Shen gui head, reptile. Reptile head. Snake, gecko, turtle? Full dark green face at angle. Red bushy brows, round eyes, smiling red lips, teeth, yellow whiskers; ear and earring. Red area under lotus leaf hat with twin back ribbons. Small lotus bud at front.
