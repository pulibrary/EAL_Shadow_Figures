type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Lao Chou. Manchu. Round blue hat with wide brim, black cut circle at crown and cut decos on brim; short red ribbon at nape; white scalp and neck. Small white tufted brow, sideburns to stachio and two strand beard, soul patch. Peach flesh face, cut wrinkles; fish eye; cut teeth, tongue, lips.
dimensions
H: 3 ½ x 2 ¾.
