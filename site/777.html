description
Gentry, child body. Child body, close sleeve deco tunic over red wide trousers. Golden locket hangs conspicuously around neck. Black collar, cuffs, edging; blue ground with cut red, pink whorl flower decos; block hands; black hem with cut flower and leaf deco in natural, red, green; red shoes with black, yellow cut pattern. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 6 ½ x 4; neck rod 11; hand rods 9 ¼, 9.
