45
Chou head. Wu chou. Minister. Flesh face; white clown patch, black edged, red closed lips. High hexagonal black hat sits forward on head; has large black topknot, small red pompom pairs, natural repeat decos, black ciguye 慈菇葉 with 3 small red pompoms; short red ribbon at nape; large red pompom at ear. Lacquered.
46
Chou head. Wu chou. Minister. Black, open cut face; white clown patch, red lips, teeth. High hexagonal black hat, large black topknot, small red pompom pairs, natural repeat decos, black ciguye 慈菇葉 with 3 small red and 2 black pompoms; short red ribbon at nape. Lacquered.
47
Sheng head. Wu sheng. Minister. White flesh face, pink blush; full grey filigree cut opera style beard covers mouth. High yellow ying luomao 硬羅帽 hexagonal hat with blue cloud repeat decos; black decos and topknot, 1 large black pompom above diadem, ezi 額子 in natural, red, green, and near black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow; short red back ribbon. No lacquer.
