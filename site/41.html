Armor female. Woman warrior, wu dan 武旦. Martial attire, ruan kao 軟靠. Black, dark blue above knee tunic, cut out flowered pattern, close fitting sleeves, white cross chest tie with back sash, front extending sash from waist, back waist sash, white with blue lining, red balloon pants, red shoes, bound feet; hands each inscribed fists. 3 rods.
