description
Altar with Potions property. Black table with four ochre cups with blue rims, black hinged flaps open or close to reveal or not these cups. Red bunting at front edge of table with blue decos. A large wooden bucket and large wooden barrel stand at either side of table, partially obscured by red lacquer pillars supporting a high cross-beam and two smaller beams for deco. Blank framed ochre placard hangs at an angle, as in imperial hall entries. Blue, red, symmetrical decos hang from tops of pillars.

H: 9 ¾ x 11.
