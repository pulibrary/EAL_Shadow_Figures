Jing head. Wu jing. Deep green cloth bao jin 包巾 hat, black martial arrow; 1 large red pompom; front emblem on blue diadem, ezi 額子, 8 red pompoms on 4 natural verticals, black decos. Flesh face, swoosh cuts, heavy black greasepaint stachio covers mouth. Zi barely visible on neck: Lacquer.
