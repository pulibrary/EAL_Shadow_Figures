type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Lao wu chou. Orange cloth bao jin 包巾 hat with blue green cross; 8 small red pompoms atop partly obscured verticals. Blue, green, yellow cut diadem, ezi 額子, front emblem, 1 large natural pompom near ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Wrinkled, flesh face; clown patch extends over nose, black marks, fishtail eye; lips; natural, strand cut beard at jaw line, 'ba' 八 stachio, soul patch. Lacquer.
dimensions
H: 4 ¼ x 2 ¾ .
