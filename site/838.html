Military male body. Yellow, wide sleeve short, front button jacket, flared waist, red lining; over black, close sleeve robe with blue, white Manchu cuffs; yellow front waist sash; red lined robe flares back with neat pleats revealing red pantaloons tucked into black cut platform boots; block hands. 3 rods.
dimensions
