1920
Chou head. Wu. Chou. Flesh face, clown patch covers nose; black marks and swoosh brow. Black triangle eye. Red lips outlined in black; teeth, tongue. Red ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four verticals, altogether 7 small red pompoms at top and middle. Large red pompom at ear. Circlet with 1 large red pompom atop central pompom ornament. Lacquered, natural neck.
