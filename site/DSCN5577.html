Jing head. Wu jing. Black painted face, natural, black highlights; full black opera-style beard and stachios cover mouth. 3 plugs attach beard at time of making. Red wind cap 風帽. Red close fitting, pattern edge, green back tie; ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow.
79
Chou head. Chou. Flesh face, black highlights near clown patch; triangle eye; black edged smiling red lips, teeth. Red close fitting wind cap 風帽, flower pattern edge,back pleats; green back tie.
80
Sheng head. Sheng official. Black open cut face; long black stachios and beard strands. Red wind cap 風帽 concealing black gauze shamao 紗帽 hat. Oblong decorated fin visible at ear. Lacquer.
