type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Chou 丑. Domestic or servant. Natural eye patch covers nose, stylized diamond shape black eye; black lines cross cut away cheek; two strand, sideburns into straight black to grey beard, stachio, soul patch; red lips, teeth. Jia ren jin 家人巾, soft black cloth hat rests forward, black top knob; green fillet; red neck ribbon. No lacquer.
dimensions
H: 4 x 2 ½
