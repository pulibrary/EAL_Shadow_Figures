Chou head. Chou official. Black open cut face; amused clown eye; black ‘ba’ 八 stachios, soul patch, and beard. Rumpled red wind cap 風帽 concealing shamao sitting forward on brow. Lacquer.

Sheng head. Wu sheng. Black open cut face, long black stachios and beard strands end in points. Red close fitting wind cap 風帽, cut patterned edge, green back tie; 2 small pompoms on martial arrow, ciguye 慈菇葉. Lacquer.
86
Jing head. Wu jing. Red painted face, natural, black highlights; full black opera-style beard and stachios cover mouth. 3 plugs attach beard at time of making. Red close fitting wind cap 風帽, cut pattern edge, green back tie; black martial arrow ciguye 慈菇葉.
