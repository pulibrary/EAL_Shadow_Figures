664
Equestrian scholar. Flesh face, round red hat, black brim with front ochre emblem; pair ochre cut flower sprays stuck upright in hat; dark green wide sleeve gown, red, green collar trim; cut wave pattern sleeve cuffs and hem; black painted waves and mountains on ochre chest piece; astride red horse, black mane, ornamental trappings; black boots in stirrups. ¾ view. Only horse’s legs move.
