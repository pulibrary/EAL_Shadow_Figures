type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing. Marquis. Hou mao 候帽 or Er bu wen 耳不聞 hat. Large red pompom above front emblem and blue, pink stripped edge; high top knot; in red, pink, green, natural, ochre, black, grey. Ochre hexagonal filigree pattern on side panel of helmet. Red painted face, black, white above eye; full black opera style beard covers mouth. Unvarnished. Note: beard made of two pieces carefully sewn together.
