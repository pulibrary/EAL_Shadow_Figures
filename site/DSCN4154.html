1058
Chou head. Jing or chou. Manchu. Round dark grey hat with wide brim, blue cut decos at crown and upper edge of brim; short red ribbon at nape; grey scalp. White flesh face with pink blush; black fish eye and brow.
1059
Dan flip-face head. Dan and dead or sleeping dan. Black snood edged with white sawtooth border and cut curved shapes covers black hair from ear back. Black fringed brow and cut face, open eye hinged to black fringed brow and flesh face, closed eye. Seems to be open to closed eye mode.
1060
Sheng head. Lao sheng. Zhanmao 氈帽 soft felt hat in light grey with small fishtail crown and fringe, close fitting upturned brim; black band visible at forehead; short red ribbon at nape. Filigree cut white side burn connected to opera style stachio and long beard. Cut white face with pale pink blush at cheek and above.
