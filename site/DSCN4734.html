Chou Dan head. Chou. Wu. Female rebel. Flesh face, cut clown patch. Black hair fringe bangs, long, red bound Suzhou style red bound hair extends above partially obscured plait; Green edge cut diadem, ezi 額子, large red pompom near ciguye 慈菇葉, twin pheasant feathers, natural white furs extend back.
2209
Dan head. Dan. Female rebel chief. 反王帽. Woman warrior. Black, open cut face. Black hair fringe bangs. Green edge cut diadem, ezi 額子, large red pompom near ciguye 慈菇葉, red flower at ear; small earring. Large green pompom at square deco cut crown, black, red back cloth. Twin pheasant feathers, natural white furs extend back.
