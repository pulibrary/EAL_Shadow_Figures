type
jing_head
description
JIng head. Wu jing demon. Rebel military commander. Natural cut diadem, ezi 額子, green trim, with red and green front emblem. Blue ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with yellow twin mirror lightning bolts, with 6 small red pompoms; green back edge. 1 large red pompom and black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Twin pheasant feathers protrude from red and green settings above ears.Twin natural furs extend back. Pink painted face, white streak above black stylized eye; black brow, full opera style beard covers mouth. Unlacquered.
