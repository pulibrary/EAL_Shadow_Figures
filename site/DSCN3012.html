1117
Chou head. Lao chou. Military. Black wangzi 網子 skullcap with green edges; short red ribbon at back and at knob. Large white filigree cut faji 髮髻 hair knob, protrudes at crown. Attached when created with two rawhide plugs. White cut face; clown patch; white filigree cut hair and short beard connects to sideburns, ’ba‘ 八 stachio. Small red lips. Ochre color from varnish.
