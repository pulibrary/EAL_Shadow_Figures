Chou head. Lao wu chou. Orange cloth bao jin 包巾 hat with blue green cross; 8 small red pompoms atop partly obscured verticals. Blue, green, yellow cut diadem, ezi 額子, front emblem, 1 large natural pompom near ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Open cut, natural, wrinkled face; clown patch, fishtail eye; teeth, lips, tongue; natural, strand cut beard at jaw line, 'ba' 八 stachio, soul patch. Lively expression. Lacquer.
