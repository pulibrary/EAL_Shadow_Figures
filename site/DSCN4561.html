1798
Jing head. Jing. Demon. Red Liu Hai style hair forms points below ears, also cascades down back, ear tufts; ochre knob protrudes from top of head; green ear ring. Green edge ochre diadem, ezi 額子, ochre caltrop, green emblem, green pompom above forehead. Red, orange, pink cut painted face; red downturned lips, teeth, tongue; natural neck.
1799
Jing head. Jing. Demon. Red to pink Liu Hai style hair forms point below ears, also cascades down back, ear tufts, twin point long beard, pointed stachio, soul patch; bat shaped brow; green, red knob or sword hilt with yellow knotted tassel protrudes from top of head; pale green ear. Green circlet, ochre caltrop. Green cut painted face; red, natural decos; lips, teeth, tongue; natural neck.
1800
Jing head. Jing. Demon. Red open twist style hair knots, forms points below ears, Liu Hai style points down back, ear tufts. Ochre circlet, ochre caltrop. Green, blue, ochre cut deco painted face; red downturned lips; natural neck.
