jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing demon. Black wangzi 網子 skullcap with short red ribbon at back. Green circlet edge and green edged circle at crown, red bound small hair knob protrudes here. Black to grey ear tuft. Pale pink face with black diamond patch stylized eye, grey mark near stachio. Two strand long natural ochre beard, soul patch, tufted brow. Small red lips. Ochre color from varnish.
dimensions
H: 4 x 2 ½.
