2202
Yao head centipede. Martial spirit of centipede. Red, yellow, blueish, black painted face, Kiss band member. Black crescent over eye. Natural diadem, ezi 額子, front emblem, black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow, 1 large red pompom. Twin pheasant feathers and furs extend back. Red ear tuft points back. Pointed red sideburn style beard and stachios, soul patch; teeth, tongue, lips. Red, green, black, natural winged centipede on back of hair knobs.
2203
Dan head, Black Fox Spirit. Martial dan head, black fox spirit. Shen gui. Black open cut face. Natural diadem, ezi 額子, front emblem, black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery arrow, profile black fox stands on 4 large red tone pompoms, red tone floral deco at ear; faded pink wind cap; twin pheasant feathers and natural furs extend back.
