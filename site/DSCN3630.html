1422
Jing head. Jing. Wu. Demon. Military secretary. Flesh face, blush on cheek; heavy black eye; black greasepaint stachio, sideburns, black ear tuft. 中軍帽 red hat with spire, decorated, colored, cut; red ornament, wide red brim.
1423
Chou head. Lao chou Manchu. Yamen runner. Red round hat with upturned black brim; cut pheasant feather hangs back from red topknot. Flesh face, small white ’ba‘ 八 stachio and soul patch; hinged white queue. Note: feather beautifully cut.
1424
Chou head. lao chou Manchu. Yamen runner. Red round hat with upturned black brim; cut pheasant feather hangs back from red topknot. Cut flesh face, small 'ba' 八 stachio, soul patch, short filigree pointed beard; hinged grey to black queue.
