jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing rebel. Blue helmet, variation on 944 with twin pheasant feathers and natural furs extending back. 2 large red pompoms, 2 small red pompoms atop green mirror thunderbolts. Pink, yellow, black, red, natural ciguye 慈菇葉. Red to pink painted face, blue and white cut decorations, stylized black eye, long, full ertiao 二挑, pointed stachio, soul patch, teeth; no lacquer.
