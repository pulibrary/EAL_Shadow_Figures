962
Dan head. Wu dan. Phoenix diadem and helmet. Three profile phoenix with red tassels in beaks. Natural cut diadem, ezi 額子. Square filigree helmet with large red pompom; twin upward decs at back; twin pheasant feathers cascade back over natural twin fur tails; natural back cloth with half red flower deco; cut earring. Black hair; flesh face, large pink rouged cheek; determined look.
963
Chou head. Chou porter or official. Black scholar’s hat with top bent over showing green serrated edge; large red circle half visible on top; jade emblem above forehead; green band ends with short red back neck ribbon. Shaved green head. Clown patch, triangle eye, black marks on nose, brow and lining around red closed lips.
964
Sheng head. Sheng. Scholar official. White painted face, pink rouge. Cut, fringe of hair at forehead. Black gauze hat with small red ribbon at crown and neck; jade square above brow; twin vertical natural and green flowers behind ears; oblong deco patterned fins in grey, red, green. Natural neck.
