jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu. Jing. Military. Red pink, white, black painted face; yellow circle on forehead; stylized black eye; full black opera style beard covers mouth. Ying zajin 硬紮巾 style hat with three rows of small pink to red pompoms above natural, ochre, green, red cut diadem, ezi 額子. Front emblem, ciguye 慈菇葉 with three small green and two small red pompom decos. 4 red pompoms atop 4 green vertical sticks, blue back upward cloth. Natural neck.
