Dan head. Flesh face wu dan. Black close fitting net helmet; large red pompom atop and four small blue pompoms around front emblem in white, red, green; green ruyi cloth, red flower and tassels in red, green at ear; small roundel at back of white crane holding red, green bead tassel in beak; front and back cut gauze extensions; small ear ring.
