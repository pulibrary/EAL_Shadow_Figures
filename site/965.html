type
dan_head
description
Dan head. Wu dan general. Natural diadem, ezi 額子, black bravery emblem; red front and ear emblems, blue grey edge and large pompom. Twin pheasant feathers cascade back and meet twin natural fur tails. Hair bound at back in horse tail curve with cut red ribbon; slim braid separates fur tails. Cut face in black; small purple ear ring.
dimensions
H: 5 ¾ x 4.
