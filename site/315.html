Jing head. Wu jing. Dark pink zajin 紮巾, 4 green verticals, 7 small red pompoms; deco diadem, ezi 額子, front ornament, ciguye 慈菇葉 with 2 small green, 2 small red pompom decos; 3 rows blue tone small pompoms. White painted face, black, yellow patch at nose bridge; heavy black stylized eye; full black opera beard covers mouth.
