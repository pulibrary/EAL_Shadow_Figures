980
Jing head. Wu jing. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with red tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large red pompom in front of profile head of blue bird, large green pompom behind it; red, blue and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with green cut ‘shou’ 壽 on red ground, green edge. White painted face, red mark from forehead to nose, grey brow, black stylized eye; opera style beard painted with black stripes; flesh visible behind.
981
Sheng head. Wu sheng. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with red tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large red pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with black cut ‘shou’ 壽 on red ground, black edge. Cut face in black; long cut stachios mix with cut beard.
982
Jing head. Wu jing. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with red tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large black pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red, blue, yellow and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with yellow cut ‘shou’ 壽 on blue ground, black edge. Black painted face with white above eye and fading to grey on cheek and above brow; full black opera style beard covers mouth.
