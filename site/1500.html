description
Jing head. Jing. Chongtian guan 沖天冠. White painted face with small grey swoosh marks, single elongated mark below triangle eye. Full grey opera style beard covers mouth. Grey is made of thin black lines painted on beard. Profile blue dragon stands between 2 yellow pompoms on hat. Cut decos in red, green yellow, black, green; yellow tassel at back.
