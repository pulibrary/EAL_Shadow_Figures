type
male_body
description
Military male body. Pink ground close sleeve, v-neck tunic, grey and blue cut whorl pattern throughout, over green cloth. Natural front tie waist sash. Pink trousers tuck into cut black, blue, green, grey boots with butterfly and fish deco. Fists. 3 rods. See Simon, p. 82 example 57 “Hose und Jackett des Leibwächters. Passender Kopf – 5.27.80 (Abb.).”
dimensions
H: 6 ¼ x 4 ¼; neck rod 10 n1/4; hand rods 8 ¾.
