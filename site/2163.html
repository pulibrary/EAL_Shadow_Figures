Yangge cart, yahuan property. 2 chou, 2 sheng yahuan 丫鬟 stand facing forward in a card drawn by a bored looking white horse with black mane and sadle. 2 chou, small waist drums. 2 sheng, hand held red baton and natural drum head. Natural cart open at front and rear, looks like wooden slats. Large hinged wheel with black bosses. Horse hinged to cart; heads all sewn into necks, 4 horse legs all hinged. 2 rods.
