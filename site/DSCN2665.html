1451
Dead Man, black robe. Oversize figure of a ghost, wu chang 無常. ¾ face grey complexion, tall four sided black hat; black unbound hair, mournful brows, black eyes, blood rivulets stream down cheeks from eyes; open downcast mouth, upper teeth and extended red tongue. Head inserted into black ¾ length tunic, wide sleeves, white high water pants; fists, bare feet. 1 neck rod.
