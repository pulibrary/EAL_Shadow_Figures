type
female_body
description
Gentry, female body. Deep green floppy sleeve tunic, purple long vest over red loose trousers, all with deco edges; block hands. Red cut flower on natural sleeve cuff and hem, red close sleeve and black cuff under. Blue front tie sash over vest; black cut, red quatrefoil trouser hem; bound feet in red, green blue shoes. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 ¼ x 5 ½; neck rod 12; hand rods 10 ¼.
