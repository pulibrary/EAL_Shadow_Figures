Pestle Hell Demon. Hell demon. Duidao 碓搗. Tiger skin waist cloth and boots, red accents, naked muscled torso. Hinged at waist, knees, elbow, shoulders, hands. Hands hold lacquer support. Feet attach to one end of red lacquer lever with stone pestle at other end. Profile face with horns and red ear tufts. Intent at work. Black surface of base. Hole cut in mortar and below feet.
