1120
Jing head. Lao Jing. Soldier? Black wangzi 網子 skullcap with short red ribbon at back. Pale green circlet edge, small black faji 髮髻 hair knob protrudes. Black stripe painted scalp and full opera style beard and stachios covering mouth. Natural neck; white painted face with pink blush; grey marks; black swoosh brow and triangle eye.
1121
Sheng head. Sheng. Soldier? Black wangzi 網子 skullcap with short red ribbon at back. Green circlet edge, small black faji 髮髻 hair knob protrudes at crown, bound with red cord. Black scalp. Black cut face with 'ba' 八 stachio, red lips. Ochre color from varnish.
1122
Jing head. Jing demon. Black wangzi 網子 skullcap with short red ribbon at back. Green circlet edge and green edged circle at crown, red bound small hair knob protrudes here. Black to grey ear tuft. Pale pink face with black diamond patch stylized eye, grey mark near stachio. Two strand long natural ochre beard, soul patch, tufted brow. Small red lips. Ochre color from varnish.
