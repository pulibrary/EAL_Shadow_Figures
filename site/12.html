Jing head. Wu jing. Demon. Painted face, ornate, turquoise green, white, black stylized eye; teeth; straight red beard, soul patch and stachio; red to pink ear tufts; turquoise green hexagonal minister hat, white, black accents, 32 small red to pink pompom decos, turquoise green top knob; black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow with small red and turquoise green pompom decos; short red neck ribbon. No lacquer.
