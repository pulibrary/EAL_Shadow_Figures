Jing head. Jing. Daoist. 神仙. Natural white, blush painted face; black stylized eye and brow. Red dot on forehead; black greasepaint style beard covers mouth; Liu Hai style black hair, front fringe, long pointed sideburns, smooth hair extends back; green circle on head. Ochre circlet, caltrop front emblem. Natural neck.
