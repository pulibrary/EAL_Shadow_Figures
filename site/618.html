618

type
figure
description
Crawling male. Open cut chou; teeth, tongue; blue harness and bit; black gauze hat front green tie; red ¾ sleeve tunic and matching pants; hands and fingers, forearms, bare feet and ankles revealed; natural saddle, red lining, blue green girth. Hinge at one shoulder, waist; all limbs in bent posture. 1 rod.
dimensions
H: 5 ½ x 8 ½; pants rod 10 ¼.
