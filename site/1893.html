Jing head. Jing. 神仙. Daoist. Green painted face, white; heavy black eye. Red repeats at brow area. Red to pink down pointing ear tuft, pointed twin sideburns to stachio, soul patch. Lips, teeth, tongue. Prominent faceted diadem, ezi 額子, cut green edge, red ground, quatrefoil deco. Natural ochre back elements with profile lotus; tassel. Natural neck.
