jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing commander. Stiff black gauze ying zajin 硬紮巾; natural, red, green diadem, ezi 額子, with central ornament topped by large black pompom and black bravery arrow ciguye 慈菇葉; back ‘ruyi’ diadem topped by large black pompom; twin mirror natural lightning bolts topped by small black pompoms; twin natural up pointed ornaments at back. Menacing black, grey, white face, downward red lips. Black beard and hair. Lacquered.
