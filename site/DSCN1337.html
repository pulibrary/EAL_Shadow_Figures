1766
Chou head. Lao chou. Demon. Daoist. Open cut natural face; clown patch, amused eye, wrinkles. Opera style natural filigree beard, stachio, soul patch; back pointing ear tuft; lips, teeth, tongue. Tall black with red, yellow cut deco hat, topped by folded Daoist cap, pleated black. Hat sits forward on head.
1767
Chou head. Lao chou. Daoist. Open cut wrinkled natural face; clown patch, amused eye. Natural color tufted brow, opera style natural filigree beard, stachio, soul patch; back pointing ear tuft; lips, teeth, tongue. Black with red, green, yellow cut deco folded Daoist hat; twin back elements. Hat sits back on head.

Sheng head. Lao sheng. White painted face, blush. Natural opera style filigree beard covers mouth. Wind cap over shamao. Blue cloth with red lining drapes over close fitting cap, green edge, red tone flower.

Dan head. Guanyin. Wind cap over shamao. White painted face, blush. Blue cloth with red lining drapes over close fitting cap, green edge, mustard flower repeats; large red tone flower, back piece shows Buddha image seated on lotus.
