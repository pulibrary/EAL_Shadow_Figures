type
figure
description
Musician Drummer. Chou Manchu pedestrian hits horizontal drum, strapped over shoulders. Profile, grey black face, cut eye; red Manchu porter’s hat with top knot and black brim, queue partially visible; red wide sleeve, stiff green collar, tunic over black close sleeve tunic; green cuffs and pants tuck into black boots. Both hands hold green sticks over rawhide drum face, round holes indicate brass tacks onto red lacquer body. Movable legs only. 1 rod at neck.
dimensions
H: 11 x 5 ½; drum 2 x 2 ¾; rod 11 ¼.
