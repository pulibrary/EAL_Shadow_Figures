700
Chou head. Lao chou, flesh cut face, flesh fine cut hair, beard, brow and stachio; subtle clown patch. Yuan wai jin; black ground, green edging and circlet; on sides, cut flower resembles cut quatrefoil with red center at top; prominent cut flower above brow; twin deco cloths hang from upper back corner; short red back tie.
701
Sheng head. Lao sheng, open cut face, natural fine cut hair, opera beard and stachio. Yuan wai jin 員外巾; black ground, blue green edging and circlet; cut red starburst on sides; cut quatrefoil with red center at top; cut white square, blue green edge above brow; twin triangles of deco cloth hang from upper back corner; short red back tie.
702
Sheng head. Lao sheng, open cut face, natural cut opera beard and stachio. Yuan wai jin 員外巾; black ground, ochre edging and circlet; cut red starburst on sides; cut quatrefoil with red center at top; cut ochre square, same edge above brow; twin triangles of deco cloth hang from upper back corner; short red back tie.
