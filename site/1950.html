description
Jing head. Wu. Jing. Natural white painted face; black brow; grey forehead; thin black stripes on cheek and chin; heavy black eye; frowning red lips outlined in black. Black ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four natural white verticals, 4 small black pompoms at top and 4 small natural white ones at middle. One large black pompom atop central green pompom ornament, and large black pompom at flesh ear; black pate, natural neck.
