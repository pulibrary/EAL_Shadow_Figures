Sheng head. Wu. Sheng. Flesh face, pale blush; long black opera style stachios, sideburns end in three points; cover mouth. Yellow ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four green verticals, 8 small blue pompoms at top and at middle. One large blue tone pompom atop central pompom ornament. Pale green pate, natural neck.
