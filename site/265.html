type
creature
description
Red snake animal. Natural with red back, teeth, flame-like tongue extended, round black eyes; movable body features 52 hinged plates sewn together with thick white thread to pointed tail. 2 rods. Could go with Madame White Snake.
dimensions
H: head 1 ¾ x 3 ½; L body 25 ¼. Head rod 10 ¼; tail rod 6 ¾.
