974

type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Wu chou. Military. Trying to look like a jing. Stiff black gauze hat; natural, red, green diadem, ezi 額子, with central ornament topped by large red pompom and black bravery arrow having its own tiny red and blue green pompoms; central large red pompom; twin mirror natural lightning bolts; half cut flower in red, blue, green on hat; twin faded yellow up pointed ornaments at back; faded yellow neck shield deco. White face, large clown patch edged in pale pink; black brow, triangle eye, mark on cheek; black greasepaint style stachio with fuzzy edge covers mouth.
dimensions
H: 3 ½ x 3 ¼.
