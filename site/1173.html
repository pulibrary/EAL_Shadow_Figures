type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing commander. Ying zajin 硬紮斤 in blue, black, natural, green, grey, black, yellow with cut green edge; single red, white, blue green floral deco with large red and green pompoms above it; yellow emblem at nape and red cut hanging ornament. Mirror white lightning verticals topped by two small black pompoms. Natural cut diadem, ezi 額子, behind floral deco matches front diadem with green cut edge, red and green central emblem topped by large pompom; black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Black opera style full beard, stachio covers mouth. Red painted face; white streak above black brow and eye; natural neck. Lacquered.
