sheng_head
description
Sheng head. Lao sheng, open cut face, natural fine cut hair, opera beard and stachio. Yuan wai jin 員外巾; black ground, blue green edging and circlet; cut red starburst on sides; cut quatrefoil with red center at top; cut white square, blue green edge above brow; twin triangles of deco cloth hang from upper back corner; short red back tie.
dimensions
H: 4¼ x 2 ¾.
