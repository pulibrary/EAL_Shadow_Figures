type
dan_head
description
Chou dan head. Chou Dan or Chou hua dan. Black fringed bangs high on forehead and around nape. Congyoubing 蔥油餅 style bun at nape with two yellow clasps, and three white ornaments with green, yellow decos, bound with red cord at two places; tiny rows of red, green pompoms above it; pink profile flower with leaves and four pink, green extensions at ear; small green ear ring. Pale face, pink blush from brow to cheek. Black marks on nose and face; stylized downward looking eye.
dimensions
H: 2 ½ x 2 ½.
