Chou head. Chou. Flesh face, subtle clown patch; black brow, Dali stachios, soul patch, three point opera style long stachio and pointed sideburns. Lips, teeth, tongue. Black shamao hat with fins, tilted forward. Red ribbons tied at top, draped from fins. Natural neck, black hair bound at nape with red ribbon, black pate. Possibly City God 城隍.
