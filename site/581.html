type
creature
description
Horse animal. Dappled grey, black mane, tail, hooves; hinged knees, tail with string mechanism to limit default angle; blue reins, red tassels; red, white saddle, blue cut out flower on white saddle blanket, black edging. Single chopstick, painted red, green, split half way, horse inserted and knotted securely at two places with white cotton thread. Thicker rawhide.
dimensions
H: 8 ½ x 12; chopstick 10.
