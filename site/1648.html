Jing head. Jing demon. Daoist? Red hair falls back in three layers, ear tuft and long spike of hair as sideburn. Ochre circlet with caltrop crescent shape as emblem. Grey circle at top of head. Blue painted face with red highlights; black scallop shaped stylized eye; frowning red lips and five o’clock shadow around mouth; natural neck.
