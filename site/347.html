description
Sheng head. Wu sheng. Pale green cloth bao jin 包巾 hat; 1 large natural pompom atop red, green, blue front emblem with 4 small natural pompoms, green edge; 8 natural pompoms on 4 blue verticals, black decos. Blue ribbon extends past flowerlike deco at ear. Natural face, blush. No lacquer makes 'natural' almost white color.
dimensions
H: 4 ¾ x 2 ¾.
