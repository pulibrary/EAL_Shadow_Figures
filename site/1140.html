type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing. Military. Natural zhanmao 氈帽 felt hat with fishtail crown, deco of profile red to pink peony-like flowers and green leaves; green circlet with large black wavy style arrow or ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol; short red ribbon at nape. Natural pompom at ear. Red painted face with black and white highlights at eye. Black scalp; pink ear; natural neck.
dimensions
H: 3 ½ x 3.
