type
male_body
description
Imperial robe, male body. Black wide sleeve, front button dragon robe. Half visible frontal dragon faces and curled bodies at chest and waist; wide sleeve cuffs and hem of stripped sea and waves with stylized flowers and possibly mountains. Decos in red, yellow, blue. Block hands; yellow leggings and simple black cotton shoes. Coiled metal at hand end of hand rod indicates special uses possible. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 ½ x 5 ½; neck rod 12; hand rod 6 ¼.
