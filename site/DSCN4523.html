1325
Jing head. Jing demon. Official. White painted face with black stripes on cheek, frowning red lips; black stylized eye; red mark on forehead; natural neck. Black-green, close fitting wang mao hat with red tassel at back; cut decos in green, natural, red, black. Front emblem on small diadem, ezi 額子; 2 large red pompoms.
1326
Jing head. Jing demon. Official. Red painted face with black stripes on cheek, frowning red lips; black stylized eye; yellow mark on forehead; natural neck. Black-green, close fitting wangmao 王帽 hat with red tassel at back; cut decos in green, natural, red, black. Front emblem on small diadem, ezi 額子; 2 large red pompoms.
1327
Jing head. Jing. Official. Red painted face with round yellow coin on cheek; yellow, black, white accents on forehead area. Black stylized eye; bull black opera style beard covers mouth; natural neck. Yellow, close fitting wangmao 王帽 hat with red tassel at back; cut decos in green, red. Front emblem on green edge; 1 large yellow pompom.
