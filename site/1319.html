jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing. Wu. Demon. Rebel warrior. Black, natural deco cut painted face, stylized eye; black pointed beard from jowels, shaven chin, soul patch, stachio. Kiss band member. Hexagonal hat with central knob, ying luomao 硬羅帽; cut decos in natural, green. Front diadem, ezi 額子, central emblem, black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol; 1 large black pompom; twin pheasant feathers; twin natural furs hang back.
