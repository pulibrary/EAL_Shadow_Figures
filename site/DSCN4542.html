1567
Sheng head. Sheng. Crown prince. White painted face, pale blush at eye and cheek; grey shaved head. Wide white diadem, ezi 額子, with blue profile dragon and red pompom over large ornate front rosette and small pompoms; red tassel extends from ear. Small crown at top of head in red with green, yellow, black highlights; large red pompom; red filigree back ornament extends from horizontal yellow ornament.
1568
Sheng head. Sheng. Eunuch. White painted face, pale pink blush at eye to cheek. Black close fitting hat with yellow profile dragon; decos in red, green, natural; green diadem, ezi 額子, two large red pompoms.
1569
Jing head. Jing. High official or king. Close fitting hat in yellow with green deco uprights and fillet; green profile dragon at side. Large front rosette, large yellow pompom. Red painted face with red markings in white swath above eye; white filigree full opera style beard and stachio covers mouth.
