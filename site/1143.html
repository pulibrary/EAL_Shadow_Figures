1143

type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing demon. General. Natural cut diadem, ezi 額子, green trim, with red and green front emblem. Natural pompom above; large twisted blue fan-shaped symbol at front. Twin pheasant feathers protrude from red and green settings above ears. Black to grey cascading shapes on back of head; black Liu Hai 劉海 knots behind diadem. Twin natural furs extend back. Long piece in front of ear. Black full beard starts at chin. Flesh colored ear ring. Slight ridges on forehead. White painted face with black curving patterns highlight round black eye. Cut white teeth and pointed fangs, red tongue, pink lips.
dimensions
H: 5 ¼ x 4 ¼.
