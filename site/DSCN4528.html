1340
Sheng head. Lao sheng. Open cut face; natural color, filigree cut full opera style beard and top knot protruding through black faji 髮髻 close fitting hat.
1341
Jing head. Jing. Wu. Red painted face, yinyang symbol on forehead. Full black opera style beard covers mouth; nearly invisible plugs attach it to self half way down its length. Black faji close fitting hat, green edge, ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Natural neck.
1342
Sheng head. Wu. Lao sheng. White painted face, pink blush around eye and forehead areas. Pointed opera style beard and stachios cover mouth, natural color. Black faji close fitting hat, black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Natural neck.
