Jing head. Wu jing. Military. Wide brim caomao quan 草帽圈 straw hat with green and black cut edges. Black cut hexagonal hat with white edges and deco at top, large black topknot. Black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Blue circlet; red pompom at ear. Green, red, white, black painted face, kiss band member. Black stylized eye and lip outline. Red downcast lips. Natural neck.
