Chou head. 小丑 xiao chou rebel king. Crown, red with green cut edge, red tassel, black back cloth, twin pheasant feathers, natural furs. 2 large red pompoms, ciguye 慈菇葉, diadem, ezi 額子. Flesh face, triangle black eye and marks on nose and corners of red, open mouth, teeth, tongue; lacquered natural neck.
