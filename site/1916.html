description
Chou head. Wu. Chou. Black open cut face; clown patch. Red lips, teeth, tongue. Black ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four green verticals, altogether 7 small red pompoms at top and middle. Diadem, ezi 額子, with 1 large red pompom and black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Lacquered natural neck.
