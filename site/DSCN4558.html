1789
Sheng head. Sheng. Deity. Black open cut face, 'ba' 八 stachios, soul patch, short pointed beard. Blue circlet with uppointed red tassel in front of blue profile dragon. Black close fitting cap with large stiff angled ribbons tied in red; back cloth.
1790
Sheng head. Sheng. Buddhist. Black open cut face. Round blue and black cut deco hat with cut button top knot; wide red upturned brim with scalloped edge in cut yellow. Elegant.
1791
Sheng head. Sheng. King. Open cut face, black. Chongtian guan 沖天冠 crown. Blue profile dragon standing on hat between two natural pompoms. Central ornament. Natural tassel at back; cut decos in black, grey, red, blue black, green, natural.
