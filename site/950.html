type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Wu chou. Stiff black gauze hat; natural, red, green diadem, ezi 額子, with central ornament topped by large red pompom and black bravery arrow; back ‘ruyi’ diadem topped by large red pompom; twin mirror natural lightning bolts topped by small red pompoms; twin yellow up pointed ornaments at back; small yellow neck guard ornament. Black stylized brow and marks on face; large white clown patch, black fish eye; opera style drooping black stachio and side beards; cut teeth, tongue, lips.
dimensions
H: 3 ¼ x 3.
