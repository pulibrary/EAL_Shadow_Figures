Jing head. Jing. Caomao quan 草帽圈 broad brimmed natural cloth or straw hat with black cut edge; open at top of crown for zhanmao 氈帽 soft felt hat in light grey with large fringed fishtail crown, deco of cut yellow and green butterfly on green plant; green fillet with black ciguye 慈菇葉. White painted face with pink blush on forehead and below black stylized eye; white from black brow and eye over nose; full, black opera-style beard and stachio covers mouth.

H: 5 x 3.
