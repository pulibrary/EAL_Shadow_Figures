2211
Dan head. Dan. Female rebel chief. 反王帽. Woman warrior. Black, open cut face. Black hair fringe bangs. Small black profile dragon between two natural white pompoms on crown. Green and red alternating cut edge, front emblem, flower, cut natural elements; small earring. Twin pheasant feathers, natural white furs extend back.
2212
Dan head. Dan. Female rebel. 反鳳冠. Woman warrior. Black, open cut face. Black hair. Natural white cut diadem, ezi 額子, three profile bird heads, each beak holds red tassel; small earring. Profile natural white lotus-like flower above crown; deco back ornament; twin pheasant feathers, natural white furs extend back.
