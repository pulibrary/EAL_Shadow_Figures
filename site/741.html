male_body
description
Gentry, male body. White ground wide sleeve robe, large red pink peony like stem decos, red lining; fists; red close cuff, black v-neck tunic; ochre front tie waist sash, hangs front; over black pants that tuck into black cut platform boots. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 ¼ x 5 ½; neck rod 11 ¾; hand rods 9 ¾.
