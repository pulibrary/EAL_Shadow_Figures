1709
Chou dan head. Chou dan. Deity. Flesh face, large red circle on cheek. Front ornament on circlet, earring, bound black hair, back hair ornament. Black ink on neck: 土母
1710
Jing head, flip-face. Jing demon. Knob at top of head. Scowling, round black eye, dotted area below brow; upturned hulu shape on forehead; black cut line accentuates open red lips, teeth. Hinged face, dagger held horizontally in clenched teeth, same features. Black ear tuft, layered hair falls back.
1711
Dan, chou head, flip-face. Head. Hinged face. Flesh face with cut wrinkles and brown greasepaint stachio over mouth. Hinged face, open cut dan, small red lips. Black hair coiled and bound at back of head.
