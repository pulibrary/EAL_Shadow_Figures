type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing demon. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with black tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large black pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red, yellow, blue and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with yellow cut ‘shou’ 壽 on blue ground, black edge. Black, grey, white painted face, black cut decos; black sunburst eye, pointed stachio into tufted sideburns, soul patch; cut teeth, tongue, lips.
dimensions
H: 4 ½ x 3 ¼.
