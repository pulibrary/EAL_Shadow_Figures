1675
Shen gui head, dragon. Dragon King head. Green 3/4 face, round black eyes, crossed with yellow and red aureoles. Open mouth, teeth; yellow whiskers. Red pate. Red, cut wind hat over crown in black, red, yellow; greenish.
1676
Shen gui head, bat. Demon head. Bright round black eye, natural-yellow bat covers nose and upper part of face. Nostrils are bat eyes, wings form around eye area. Green hue contrasts; red horizontal stachios, soul patch, pointed beard, close cropped hair. Small crown forward on head with large red pompom; ties at back.
1677
Jing head. Demon head. Large round black eyes; brownish face. Reddish whiskers, hair, ear tuft, soul patch, beard. Teeth, fangs. Small close fitting Daoist hat in dark colors. Face seems to express desperation.
