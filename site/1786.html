jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing. Ying shamao 硬紗帽. Prime minister or high ranking civil official. Black and white painted face, curved marks on forehead and brow area; round coin style cut in cheek. Full black beard covers mouth. Pink to red ribbon ties at hat, extends on beard with flowerlike medallion. Square green edged and black hat with profile yellow dragons. Sets of tiny twin red and bleu pompoms at ends of dragon whiskers. Decos in red, green, yellow. 2 large black pompoms. Natural neck; green pate.
