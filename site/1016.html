chou_head
description
Chou head. Lao chou. Zhanmao 氈帽 soft felt hat in grey with high brim and small fishtail at crown. Tiny tail of white hair bound and tied at back with black ribbons; grey scalp visible. Flesh face with cut wrinkles, tufted cut brow, dali stachios, soul patch, and short pointed white beard bound in two places by red ribbons; cut teeth, red tongue, pink lips.
dimensions
H: 4 x 2 ¼.
