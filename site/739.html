type
male_body
description
Off duty, male body. Light ochre ground, black grey bamboo motif, wide sleeve robe, green lining, black edge v-neck. Twin green, blue floral ‘flugel’ decos, from back to side hem, nankai chang [Simon, p. 82, see example 61]; block hands; black cut platform shoes. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 x 5 ½; neck rod 12 ½; hand rods 9 ¾.
