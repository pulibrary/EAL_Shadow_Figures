Jing head. Jing. Demon. Red, green, black highly deco painted face; black eye. Red ear tuft points down, twin red hair knots; long sideburns to stachio, soul patch below lips, teeth, tongue. Large red pompom atop cut diadem, ezi 額子; large green pompom near small bulbous crown with back elements. Lacquered.
