type
dan_head
description
Dan head. Dan. Female rebel chief. 反王帽. Woman warrior. Flesh face, blush over eye and cheek. Black hair fringe bangs. Green edge cut diadem, ezi 額子, front ornament, ciguye 慈菇葉, with 3 small pompoms, red, white, blue. Note: 2 lower ones may be broken off. Partial red flower at ear; long black extension from ear. 4 large red pompoms topped by 4 large green pompoms above diadem. Red deco cut wind hat. Twin pheasant feathers from deco holders; natural white furs extend back.
