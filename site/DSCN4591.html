1884
Chou head. Bonze. Flesh face, cut clown patch extends over nose; black triangle style eye; red lips, teeth, tongue; black swoosh style brow. Black ‘ba’ 八 stachios, soul patch, pointed beard. Shaved brown pate, natural neck. Could wear hat prop.

1886
Chou head. Chou. Demonic. Bonze. Flesh face, grey edged clown patch extends to heavy black tufted greasepaint stachio. Black stylized eye and marks; black swoosh style brow. Black cut chin. Shaved pale grey pate, natural neck. Could wear hat prop.
