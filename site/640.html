Gentry, female body. Red floppy sleeve tunic, over red loose trousers or skirt, all with deco edges in natural, black, green; block hands. Red cut flower on natural sleeve cuff; blue green close sleeve and black cuff. Green, natural, red stripe skirt or trouser, black cut sash; red quatrefoil cut back hem deco; bound feet in red, green blue shoes. 3 rods.
