type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Wu chou. Military general. Small bell-shaped yellow and black helmet with pointed spike adorned with three red tassels bent in different directions; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem, black bravery symbol, large green pompom above it; red, yellow and blue up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with green cut ‘shou’ 壽 on red ground, black edge. Cut black face with white clown patch, lively black eye; red lips; black dali stachios, soul patch, black beard.
dimensions
H: 5 x 3 ¼.
