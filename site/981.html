type
sheng_head
description
Sheng head. Wu sheng. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with red tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large red pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with black cut ‘shou’ 壽 on red ground, black edge. Cut face in black; long cut stachios mix with cut beard.
dimensions
H: 5 ½ x 4.
