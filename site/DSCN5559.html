Jing head. Wu jing. Demon. Yellow painted face, black, red, white accents; blue outlined red mouth pulled down in a grimace; red to pink ear tufts also point down below chin; turquoise green ying luomao 硬羅帽 hexagonal minister hat, white, black accents,many small red to pink pompom decos, turquoise green top knob; black martial arrow with 3 small red, 2 turquoise green pompom decos; short red neck ribbon. No lacquer.
