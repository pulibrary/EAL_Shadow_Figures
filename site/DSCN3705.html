Military winged body. Supernatural winged body of general Fei Hu Liu Qing. Red robe, cut deco of profile 4 claw dragon, multi color wave pattern hem; natural waist sash ties front; close cuffs, fists red trousers tuck into black platform boots, some deco. Top edge of wings are two rows of red to pink feathers or scales becoming black speckled green webbing. Wing pieces are made separately from arms, attached at several places by cotton [?] threads. 3 rods.
