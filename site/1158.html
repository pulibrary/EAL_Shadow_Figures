type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing. Military. Natural cut diadem, ezi 額子, with green edge, green and red front emblem, red pompom. Black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Blue cut hexagonal hat with green edges, yellow cloud-like decos, and large topknot. Twin pheasant feathers and peacock eye extend back from green, red, blue holders. Blue, red, yellow, black painted face. Red pointed stachio, soul patch and full two-strand beard. Ochre from lacquer.
dimensions
H: 5 ½ x 4 ¼.
