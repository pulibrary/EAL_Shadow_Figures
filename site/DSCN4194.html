Jing head. Demon head. Green painted face, curly red close cropped hair, brows, stachio, beard. Teeth, tongue, lips. Round black eyes, black highlights in hair. Natural neck.

Shen gui head, monkey. Monkey head. Buddhist hexa headdress with images of seated Buddha on 3 facets, back facet has bat. Yellow ingot style front emblem; side ribbon with red deco. Red painted face, round natural clown-like patch highlights eye black cut fringes face. Smiling red lips. Natural neck.

Shen gui head, monkey. Monkey head. Small lotus crown with large pompom, yellow cut front deco; red back ribbon. Red painted face, round black eye black cut fringes face. Black edged closed red lips. Small earring. Natural neck.
