1298
Jing head. Lao wen jing rebel king. Similar to 1261. Black crown, cut red flowers, 8 alternating small red and blue pompoms. Diadem, ezi 額子, with ornate central emblem, twin pheasant feathers, 2 large red pompoms, neck protector, natural furs. White painted face with prominent red stripe from forehead to nose tip, black brow and heavy black eye with pink perforated teardrop on cheek; full black manran 滿髯. Light or no lacquer on natural neck.
