type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with red tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large black pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red, blue, yellow and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with yellow cut ‘shou’ 壽 on blue ground, black edge. Black painted face with white above eye and fading to grey on cheek and above brow; full black opera style beard covers mouth.
dimensions
H: 5 x 4 ½.
