description
Fengdu City Wall property. Closed black double doors in blue green frame, red semi circle above. Pairs of bosses, in the form of 32 cut circles deco doors, 16 each door. Cloud tufts in black, red, blue green and blue surmount crenellated blue brick wall. Imposing frontal tiger face with open mouth, and poised front paws located just above door. Cut fangs, red brows, black wang 王 character on forehead.
dimensions
H: 22 ½ x 10. 2 pieces hinged together fold into 12 ¼ x 10.
