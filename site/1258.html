Chou head. Wen chou rebel official. Black shamao with round fins, furs and pheasant feathers extend back. Hat sits forward on head. Green pate; white painted face, blush, clown patch extends over triangle eye; black marks on nose, black 'ba' 八 stachio, soul patch; lips, teeth, tongue. Note hinged goatee.
