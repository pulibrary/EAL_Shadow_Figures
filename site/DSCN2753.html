Dragon robe male. Court attire, jade belt,animal face on torso, wave pattern on wide cuffs and hem. Pale green robe. One 1/2 face dragon body on chest, one profile dragon near jade belt. Wide white band at cuffs and hem with red to pink chrysanthemum prong decorations. Cut waves and mountain pattern above. Blue wings to ankle with cut decorations of pink and red. Platform boots, block hands. 3 rods. Pencil note.
