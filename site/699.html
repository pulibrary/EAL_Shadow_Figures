description
Chou head. Lao chou, open cut face, white fine cut hair, beard, brow and stachio; clown patch. Yuan wai jin 員外巾; black ground, blue green edging and circlet; cut green starburst on sides, red accents on front; cut quatrefoil with red center at top; cut white square, blue green edge above brow; twin triangles of deco cloth hang from upper back corner; short red back tie.
dimensions
H: 3 x 3 ¼.
