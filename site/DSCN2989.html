1300
Chou head. Wu chou rebel commander. Blue-green shamao with cut filigree and flower decorations in grey, pink and black; blue-green fillet and central ornament, 1 large red pompom, ciguye 慈菇葉, cut curl at back of diadem, ezi 額子, near twin pheasant feathers and natural furs. Open cut black face with horizontal black stachio, short pointed beard and soul patch, black pate; light lacquer on natural neck.
