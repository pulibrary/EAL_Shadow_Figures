818
Imperial robe, male body. Black wide sleeve, front button dragon robe. Half visible frontal dragon faces and curled bodies at chest and waist; wide sleeve cuffs and hem of stripped sea and waves with stylized flowers and possibly mountains. Decos in red, yellow, blue. Block hands; yellow leggings and simple black cotton shoes. Coiled metal at hand end of hand rod indicates special uses possible. 3 rods. Same style as blue green robe of 3.837b and black robes of 3.839b-3.840b, 3.849b. See Simon p. 83 no. 63 Longjianyi. 龍箭衣. Note that Simon’s archers have close sleeves.
