type
sheng_head
description
Sheng head. Lao sheng. Zhanmao 氈帽 soft felt hat in blue with large fringed fishtail crown; white flower and green leaves cut deco; yellow fillet with tall frontal twisted fan, could resemble ciguye 慈菇葉; square frontal emblem in white and green. Black cut repeat at fillet, short red ribbon at nape. Black cut face; think black painted lines on scalp, brow, sideburn connected to opera-style beard and stachio covering mouth.
dimensions
H: 4 ½ x 3.
