1892
Jing head. Jing. 神仙. Daoist. Ochre painted face; scalloped black eye. Red repeats at brow. Red to pink back pointing ear tuft, pointed twin sideburns to stachio, soul patch. Lips, teeth, tongue. Prominent faceted diadem, ezi 額子, cut green edge, red ground, quatrefoil deco. Natural ochre back elements with profile lotus; tassel. Natural neck.

1895
Chou head. Wu. Chou. Demon. Rebel. Large clown patch covers most of face; triangle style eye; heavy black greasepaint stachio covers mouth. Red ying luomao 硬羅帽 hexa minister hat, blue cut cloud decos. Twin pheasant feathers and peacock eye extend back from green, red, blue holders. Red pompom above natural deco diadem, ezi 額子, black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow; red neck ribbon. Lacquered.
