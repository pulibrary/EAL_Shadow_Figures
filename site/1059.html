type
dan_head
description
Dan flip-face head. Dan and dead or sleeping dan. Black snood edged with white sawtooth border and cut curved shapes covers black hair from ear back. Black fringed brow and cut face, open eye hinged to black fringed brow and flesh face, closed eye. Seems to be open to closed eye mode.
dimensions
H: 2 ½ x 2 ¼.
