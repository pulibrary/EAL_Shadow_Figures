1870
Sheng head. Sheng. Official. Black, open cut face, soul patch, three point opera style long stachios and pointed sideburns. Lips. Black shamao hat with fins. Red ribbons knotted at top, draped from fins. Short red ribbon at nape. Natural neck, black pate. Lacquered. Possibly City God 城隍.
