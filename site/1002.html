type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Wu chou. Military general. Small bell-shaped natural filigree helmet with slightly askew spike pointing forward adorned with three red tassels bent in different directions; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem, large red pompom above it; red, yellow and blue up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with yellow cut ‘shou’ 壽 on blue ground, black edge. Cut black face, white clown patch, lively black eye; black dali stachio and soul patch; cut teeth, pink tongue, red lips.
dimensions
H: 4 ½ x 3.
