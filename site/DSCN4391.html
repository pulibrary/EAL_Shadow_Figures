Milling stone property. Circular motion of black stone against base with blood oozing from between; two ovoid openings for bodies; long red handle hinged at center of stone so worker on either side can manipulate it. Two natural tabs sewn on at sides indicate there is another moving part. 1 rod. Thicker rawhide.
