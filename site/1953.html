description
Jing head. Wu. Jing. Demon. Green tone painted face; red cut repeats at brow; scalloped black eye; long red sideburns into stachio, soul patch; lips, teeth, tongue. Orange red ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four ochre verticals, 8 small green pompoms at top and at middle. One large red pompom atop central pompom ornament, and large red pompom at ear; red pate. Lacquered, natural neck.
