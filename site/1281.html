type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Wu jing rebel. Grey and filigree shamao, different maker from 1278, but same rank may be indicated. Blue, green, yellow, natural, pink to red, cut decorations. 2 large, red pompoms, ciguye 慈菇葉, twin furs and pheasant feathers. Red painted face with cut black stylized brow and crescent eye, yellow cut coin emblem on cheek. Flesh cut dot on forehead and same tone on furs and neck; full black manran 滿髯 cleverly attached with 4 plugs at time of making.
