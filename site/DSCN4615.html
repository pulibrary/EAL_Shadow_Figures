1938
Sheng head. Wu. Sheng. Flesh face, pale blush; long black opera style stachios, sideburns end in three points; cover mouth. Yellow ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four green verticals, 8 small blue pompoms at top and at middle. One large blue tone pompom atop central pompom ornament. Pale green pate, natural neck.
1939
Sheng head. Wu. Sheng. Black open cut face; long black stachios, sideburns, beard end in points; red mouth. Black ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four green verticals, 8 small red pompoms at top and at middle. One large red pompom atop central pompom ornament, and at ear. Lacquered, natural neck.
1940
Sheng head. Wu. Sheng. Black open cut face; black ‘ba’八 stachios, soul patch, pointed beard; red mouth. Red ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four ochre verticals, 8 small green pompoms at top and at middle. One large green pompom atop central pompom ornament, and large blue pompom at ear. Lacquered, natural neck.
