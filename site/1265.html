type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Wen jing. Possibly rebel demon king. Helmet and crown variation on 1261. 3 tier decorated at base of pheasant feathers, no furs. Small profile yellow dragon near diadem, ezi 額子. 1 large each red, blue pompom, also many small red and blue pompoms; front emblem. Green pate, black ear tuft or ear decoration with black tassel, black neck cloth extends back. Black, grey cut painted face, Kiss band member, downcast black outlined rubbery red lips. Lightly lacquered.
