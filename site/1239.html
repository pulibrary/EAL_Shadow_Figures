sheng_head
description
Sheng head. Wu lao sheng. Rebel commander. Orange zajin 紮巾 stiff hat with four blue/green verticals, each with 8 small red pompoms at top and middle. Diadem, ezi 額子, with 1 large white pompom and black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Twin pheasant feathers protrude from red and green settings above ears. Twin natural furs extend back. Open cut natural face, filigree white manran style beard. Lacquered natural neck.
