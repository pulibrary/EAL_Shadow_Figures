741
Gentry, male body. White ground wide sleeve robe, large red pink peony like stem decos, red lining; fists; red close cuff, black v-neck tunic; ochre front tie waist sash, hangs front; over black pants that tuck into black cut platform boots. 3 rods.
742
Gentry, male body. Green ground wide sleeve robe, large red pink peony like floral on white stem decos, pink lining; block hands; white edge v-neck; black cut platform boots. 3 rods. Note: Both hand rods have spiral metal pieces for both hands to carry something.
743
Gentry, male body. Red and ochre four petal ground wide sleeve robe, large blue peony like floral on blue green stem decos, pale green edge v-neck and lining; block hands; black cut platform boots. 3 rods.
744
Gentry, male body. Blue ground wide sleeve robe, deco of large pink plum like blossoms on cut white stems; pink lining, white edge; red v-neck robe underneath; block hands; black platform boots. 3 rods.
