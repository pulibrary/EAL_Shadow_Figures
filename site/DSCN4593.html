Jing head. Bonze. Jing. Black painted face with white cut stripe over eye, across cheek. Unsmiling red lips. Shaved natural flesh pate, neck. Could wear hat prop.
1891
Jing head. Jing. 神仙. Daoist. White, blush, black, grey, deco painted face; scalloped black eye. Black to grey back pointing ear tuft, pointed twin sideburns to stachio, soul patch. Lips, teeth, tongue. Prominent faceted diadem, ezi 額子, cut green edge, red ground, quatrefoil deco. Natural ochre back elements with profile lotus; tassel. Natural neck.
1892
Jing head. Jing. 神仙. Daoist. Ochre painted face; scalloped black eye. Red repeats at brow. Red to pink back pointing ear tuft, pointed twin sideburns to stachio, soul patch. Lips, teeth, tongue. Prominent faceted diadem, ezi 額子, cut green edge, red ground, quatrefoil deco. Natural ochre back elements with profile lotus; tassel. Natural neck.
