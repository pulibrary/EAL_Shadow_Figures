Chou head. Lao chou demon. Zhanmao 氈帽 soft felt hat in dark grey with high brim and small fishtail at crown. Grey scalp and white and black painted ear tuft, stachio, soul patch and beard; natural white face with black painted wrinkles and round red rouge dot on cheek; cut clown patch and stylized black downward gazing fish style eye; cut teeth, red tongue, pink smiling lips.
