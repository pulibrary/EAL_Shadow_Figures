Sheng head. Wu. Sheng. Commander. Yingzajin 硬紮巾 with two large white pompoms; natural opera beard and hair. Lacquered.

Jing head. Wu. Jing. Commander. Yingzajin 硬紮巾 helmet, ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow, 2 small red pompoms atop blue twin lightning bolts; 2 large black pompoms; red flower. White opera face, pale pink blush; grey brow, white opera 3 strand beard and stachio covers mouth. Unlacquered.
