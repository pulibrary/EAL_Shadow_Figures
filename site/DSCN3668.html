1165
Jing head. Wu jing general. Ying zajin 硬紮斤 in black, green, grey, yellow, red, pink decos with yellow emblem at nape above short red ribbon. Green circlet; mirror green lightning verticals topped by two small red pompoms, two small red pompoms at midpoint. Three rows of small blue to white pompoms above diadem, ezi 額子, in green and yellow; green and red round front emblem; black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol deco with two small red and three small green pompoms. Painted face in white, black, pale pink with black eye and brow; black edged yellow circle at bridge of nose; long black opera style stachio and beard covers mouth; natural neck.
1166
Chou head. Wu chou commander. Ying zajin 硬紮斤 helmet with 2 large red pompoms, ciguye 慈菇葉. Clown patch, marks on bridge of nose; dali style tufted stachios, soul patch, red lips. Lacquered.
1167
Jing head. Wu jing commander. 2 large red pompoms, ciguye 慈菇葉, ying zajin 硬紮斤. Painted face, blue to white, stylized eye area, full red 2 strand pointed beard, stachio, soul patch, teeth, lips. Kiss band member. Lacquered.
