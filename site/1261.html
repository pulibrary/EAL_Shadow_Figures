type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Lao wen jing rebel king. Light blue close-fitting helmet with small yellow crown atop, 2 small blue pompoms, twin pheasant feathers and natural furs extending back. Diadem, ezi 額子, 2 large red pompoms, central ornament with 4 small red pompoms, back ornament and red handing filigree; 3 tier decoration at base of pheasant feathers. White painted face, red stripe forhead to nose, heavy black eye, grey and black cut accents; painted black strand, manran 滿髯 full beard, light lacquer.
