Sheng head. Wu Sheng. Conical bee hive helmet. Coiled layers in red, yellow, blue. Large red pompom over front emblem and bravery symbol. Ochre ribbon ties above ear. Open cut black face, small ochre ear ring. Black hair tucked under hat. 回兵. written on neck in black ink.
