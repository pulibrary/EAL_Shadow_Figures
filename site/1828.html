Jing head. Jing. Daoist. 神仙. Red painted face, ochre deco, stylized black eye; full black beard obscures mouth. Caltrop, multi colored diadem, ezi 額子; pompoms, lotus crown and ruyi; back elements. Lacquered. Note: beard length invisibly attached by four tiny plugs.
