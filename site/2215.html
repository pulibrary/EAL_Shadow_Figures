Dan head. Dan. Female rebel. 反將. Woman warrior. Flesh face, slight blush. Black hair forehead fringe. Natural, green, ochre cut diadem, ezi 額子; profile red flower and leaves at ear; long black extension from ear. Ciguye 慈菇葉, with three small ochre, 2 small red pompoms. 4 alternating large red and green pompoms, 4 natural white pompoms. meifa 美髮 sticks partially visible in black hair; twin pheasant feathers, natural white furs extend back.
