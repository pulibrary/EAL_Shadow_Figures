Paired Equestrian Banner Bearers. Flesh face and grey face open mouth chou, seen ¾ view; red Manchu porter’s hats with top knot and black brim, queue partially visible; red, front button, stiff green collar wide sleeve tunic, black trim; each with black painted ‘xi’ 喜喜 ‘double happiness’ ochre chest roundels; each with tall, knob topped, upright red banner, fluttering scalloped edges; both wear red trousers tucked into black boots in stirrups; front natural brown steed, black mane; back natural brown steed, light mane; both hold green reins. Movable horses legs only. 1 rod.
