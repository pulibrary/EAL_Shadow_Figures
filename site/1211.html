JIng head. Wu jing demon. Stiff ying zajin 硬紮巾 green gauze hat, 3/4 back view; deco of red cut bats and clouds; yellow, red, green diadem, ezi 額子, with central ornament topped by large red pompom and black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery arrow; back ‘ruyi’ diadem topped by large black pompom; twin green lightning bolts; twin green lightning bolts. Kiss band member with stylized black eye in blue, green, red, and natural. Red pate and 2 strand opera style beard, soul patch, and single point stachio.
