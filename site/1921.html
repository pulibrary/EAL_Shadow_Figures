chou_head
description
Chou head. Wu. Chou. Flesh face, clown patch covers nose; black marks and swoosh brow. Black triangle eye. Red lips. Black ‘ba’ stachio, soul patch, pointed beard. Red ying zajin 硬紮巾 stiff hat with four green verticals, altogether 7 small red pompoms at top and middle. Large blue pompom at ear. Circlet with 1 large red pompom atop central pompom ornament. Lacquered, natural neck.
