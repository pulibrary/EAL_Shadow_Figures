Equestrian torso. Wu. Armor clad equestrian torso. Hinged arm with attached stick for securing weapon blade to its end. Cut, multicolor ying kao 硬靠 armor.
2136
Equestrian torso. Wu. Torso of armor clad equestrian general, 4 flags, hinged arm with halberd weapon attachment. This is the only half figure with the actual weapon blade attached. Halberd blade of green tone rawhide slides onto end of stick. Seam of folded rawhide is neatly sewn with thread. This halberd blade fits on other sticks but coloring matches this general's greenish armor.
