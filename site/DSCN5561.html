Chou head. Wu chou. Flesh face, white clown patch covers nose; black outlined smiling red mouth; teeth, tongue; triangle eye. Black ying luomao 硬羅帽 hexagonal minister hat, red, natural white cut flower decos, white, black accents, black top knob; red pompom at ear; black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow; short red neck ribbon. Lacquered.
32
Chou head. Wu chou. Pink painted face; black brow, cut crescents at jawline; black stylized eye; red smiling lips; grey pate; mustard color cloth back projecting hat, small top knob, natural yellow fillet ribbon with red ties at base of neck; black 慈菇葉 martial arrow. Lacquered.
33
Jing head. Wu jing. Red painted face, yellow accents on forehead to nose; heavy black brow and eye, white accent; full black opera style beard covers mouth attaches at ear; black ying luomao 硬羅帽 hexagonal minister hat and knob; green fillet; black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow; short red neck ribbon. Light lacquer.
