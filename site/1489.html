description
Sheng head. Sheng. Emperor. White painted face, pink blush at eye and cheek. Black three strand oper- style beard and stachio obscures mouth. Shuilong guan 睡龍冠. Emperor or official’s hat in yellow with green profile dragon, red, green, blue, black decos; large round front ornament, four small yellow pompoms, one large; dragons each have two small yellow pompoms; short red back ribbon. [Hat worn by the emperor outside of official business.]
