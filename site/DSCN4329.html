1718
Jing head. Jing demon. Turtle spirit. Greenish painted face, red hair, lips teeth. Circlet with ingot at front. Small profile turtle on top of head.
1719
Jing head. Jing demon. Snake spirit. Natural painted face, green snakelike lines, dots. Black hair, red circlet with ingot at front. Greenish snake on top of head.
1720
Jing head. Jing demon. Rebel general. Chicken spirit. Highly ornate black, red, grey, natural, pink painted face. Black layered hair with twin knobs. Natural white diadem, ezi 額子, ciguye 慈菇葉, large red pompom, twin fox furs, pheasant feathers. Pink profile chicken head, red comb, black accents at back of head.
