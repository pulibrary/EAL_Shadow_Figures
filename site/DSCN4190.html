1670
Shen gui head, bird. Bird head. Buddhist deity. 3/4 green face. Yellow pointed and curved beak with smiling lip. Cross eye round black eyes. Tufted cut red brows, red ear tuft and layered mane. Yellow diadem, ezi 額子, red cut deco repeats, green edge. Small lotus bud crown, cut green leaves and large pompom.
1671
Shen gui head, dragon. Dragon King head. Green 3/4 face, round black eyes, open smiling mouth, teeth; yellow whiskers. Red beard, brows, ear tufts. Small crown in black, red, yellow; large black-blue pompom.
