Deceased female body. Dan, open cut profile; black hair bound at neck and top, hanging loose; small earring; naked to waist; cords cross her sternum and upper arms, arms behind ¾ torso; red sash and bluish undergarment; bound feet, red, black, blue shoes; hinged knees; cords attached to feet indicates she hangs head down from her feet.
