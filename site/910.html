Chou head. Chou. Manchu winter court hat with upturned brim. Grey with white edging, red tri-knobbed topknot with twin grey feathers extending back. Black hinged queue, grey scalp, pink face, cut wrinkles, high tufted black brow, stachio and soul patch; downward red lips, natural neck.
dimensions
H: 5 ¾ x 3 ½.
