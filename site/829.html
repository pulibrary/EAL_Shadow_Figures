type
male_body
description
Imperial robe, male body. Black wide sleeve, front button dragon robe. Half visible frontal dragon faces and curled bodies at chest and waist; wide sleeve cuffs and hem of stripped sea and waves with stylized flowers and possibly mountains. Decos in red, natural, blue, black. Block hands; white leggings and simple black cotton shoes. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 ¼ x 5 ½; neck rod 12.
