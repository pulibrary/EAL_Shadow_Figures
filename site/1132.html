type
jing_head
description
Jing head. Jing. Military. Black wangzi 網子 skullcap with cut edges. Large black faji 髮髻 hair knob, protrudes at crown. Natural wide brim straw hat with green and blue cut edges. Green circlet with black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Black and white painted face; black starburst stylized eye; pink blush on cheek; downcast red lips outlined in black and grey.
dimensions
H: 3 ½ x 3.
