description
Chou head. Wu lao chou. Flesh tone painted face, black cut swoosh and wrinkles; triangle eye but no real clown patch; full grey opera-style beard and stachios cover mouth; grey achieved by very thin, painted black lines. 3 plugs attach beard at time of making. Red close fitting wind cap 風帽, cut pattern edge, blue back tie; black martial arrow ciguye 慈菇葉.
