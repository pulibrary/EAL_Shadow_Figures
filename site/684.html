description
Musician suona player. ¾ face Manchu pedestrian plays horizontal red, ochre, green instrument; mouth blows, big cheeks; hands on finger holes; cut eyes; red Manchu porter’s hat with top knot and black brim, queue partially visible; red wide sleeve front button tunic over black close sleeve tunic; green collar, cuffs and pants tuck into black boots. 1 rod.
dimensions
H: 9 ¾ x 5 ¼; rod 11.
