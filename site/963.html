chou_head
description
Chou head. Chou porter or official. Black scholar’s hat with top bent over showing green serrated edge; large red circle half visible on top; jade emblem above forehead; green band ends with short red back neck ribbon. Shaved green head. Clown patch, triangle eye, black marks on nose, brow and lining around red closed lips.
dimensions
H: 3 x 2¾.
