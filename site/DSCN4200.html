1695
Jing head. Jing demon. White painted face, black marks, stylized black eye ends in downward point. Black hair strand frames face, hangs back. White pompom at front, thin circlet; white half circle patch at back of head.
1696
Jing head. Jing demon. Black painted face framed in natural. Wide round eyes, fangs, teeth, lips. Black pointed beard, hair fringe at front, hanging back below neck; back pointing ear tuft. Black shamao hat with upward set fins.
1697
Shen gui head, dead. Dead head. White natural painted face, red blood drops from eyes, cheek mouth; long red tongue hangs down. Tall natural hat over black straight hair at back and strand framing face.
