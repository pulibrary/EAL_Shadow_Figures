description
Armor female. Woman warrior, wu dan 武旦. Martial attire, ruan kao 軟靠. Red deco tunic or jacket with close fitting sleeves, buttons front, dark pink turquoise green, blue, green; cut out four petal flowers, elaborate black gold-buttoned cuffs; orange cross chest tie and tassel, same waist front tie (broken in one place), long sash; blue, green cut out fish scale apron over red balloon pants; pleated turquoise green skirt or cloth with black, red deco hangs back below jacket; red lotus shoes, bound feet; block hands. 3 rods. Laquered.
dimensions
H 8 ¼; arm extended W 5 1/2; neck rod L 12 ¼; both hand rods L 10.
