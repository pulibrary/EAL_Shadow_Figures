dan_head
description
Dan head. Dan or or wu dan. Black hair in snood, bound with twin ivory meifa 美髮 sticks in figure eight at back. Elaborate ivory ties end in prominent fan at crown. Black filigree cut extensions at ear and back. Small green ear ring. White face with pale rouge. Small red lips.
dimensions
H: 4 x 2 ¾.
