Chou head. Chou. Emperor. Open cut face, clown patch; lips, teeth, tongue. Chongtian guan 沖天冠. Blue profile dragon standing on hat between two natural pompoms. Natural tassel at back; cut decos in black, grey, red, blue black, green, natural. Lacquered.
