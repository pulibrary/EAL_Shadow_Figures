Chou head. Chou. Manchu. Round dark grey hat with wide brim slightly askew, white cut circle at crown and cut deco at upper edge of brim; short red ribbons at nape; black scalp, prominent black, fringed stachio covers mouth like part of a large caterpillar. Pale flesh face with large clown patch edged in grey; triangle eye; grey and black marks; large red circle on forehead.
