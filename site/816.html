type
male_body
description
Imperial robe, male body. White dragon, front button, robe with red, green, blue, black decos; half front dragon face and curled body at chest; profile dragon at waist; semi wide sleeves and hem all have deep sea, stripped waves with flowers, clouds; carved fists from same piece as sleeve indicate this is not same maker as others in this layer; black platform shoes. Coiled metal at hand end of one hand rod indicates special uses possible. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 ¼ x 5 ¼; neck rod 12; neck rod 10.
