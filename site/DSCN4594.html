1893
Jing head. Jing. 神仙. Daoist. Green painted face, white; heavy black eye. Red repeats at brow area. Red to pink down pointing ear tuft, pointed twin sideburns to stachio, soul patch. Lips, teeth, tongue. Prominent faceted diadem, ezi 額子, cut green edge, red ground, quatrefoil deco. Natural ochre back elements with profile lotus; tassel. Natural neck.

Jing head. Jing. 神仙. Daoist. Red painted face, white, blush; heavy black eye. Black repeats at brow. Black to grey downward pointing ear tuft, pointed twin sideburns to stachio, soul patch. Lips, teeth, tongue. Prominent faceted diadem, ezi 額子, cut green edge, red ground, quatrefoil deco. Natural ochre back elements with profile lotus; tassel.
1895
Chou head. Wu. Chou. Demon. Rebel. Large clown patch covers most of face; triangle style eye; heavy black greasepaint stachio covers mouth. Red ying luomao 硬羅帽 hexa minister hat, blue cut cloud decos. Twin pheasant feathers and peacock eye extend back from green, red, blue holders. Red pompom above natural deco diadem, ezi 額子, black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow; red neck ribbon. Lacquered.
