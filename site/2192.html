Stiltwalker. Chou stiltwalker wearing tasseled wen sheng jin 文生巾 hat; hair bound in braid at back, large stachios, open cut face with large clown patch. Carries an open fan, red flowered wide sleeve lapel tunic over black trousers, black cloth shoes. Stilts attached to lower legs, hinged at knees. 1 rod at center.
