1468
Chou head. Chou. Flesh face; clown patch around downcast eye, swoosh brow. Black stachio into sideburn beard ends in two points; soul patch. Lips teeth, tongue. Natural neck. Prime minister or high ranking civil official. Wang mao; 2 large red pompoms. Profile green dragons on yellow ground. Note tiny twin red pompoms on dragon whiskers. Square style with twin deco back elements. Green edge, black, yellow, red cut decos.
