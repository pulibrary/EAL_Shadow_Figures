Jing head. Wu jing rebel demon commander. Black cut filigree decorated shamao in yellow, blue-green, red, blue. 1 each large green and red pompoms. Central ornament and ciguye 慈菇葉. Red pate, blue-green, angry looking striped cut painted face in red, pink; highly stylized black eye, white or natural oulined downcast closed red rubber lips. Light lacquer on natural neck. See 944.
