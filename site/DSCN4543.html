1570
Sheng head. Lao sheng. Non-Chinese ruler. Black cut face, oversize round eye and brow; black ‘ba’ stachio, soul patch and pointed beard. Chongtian guan 沖天冠 or wang mao 王帽. Close fitting hat in black with yellow, red, lavender decos. Red tailed profile lavender dragon on hat betweentwo large yellow pompoms; large front rosette; yellow tassel hangs back.
1571
Jing head. Jing. Emperor. Red painted face, black and white highlights; black opera style two strand beard and stachio covers mouth. Chongtian guan 沖天冠 in black, red, green, natural, grey with blue profile dragon and two large natural pompoms.
1572
Sheng head. Sheng. Emperor. Black open cut face. Chongtian guan 沖天冠 in black, red, green, natural, grey with blue profile dragon and two large natural pompoms.
