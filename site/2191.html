description
Stiltwalker. Immortal chou stiltwalker, open cut, lively clown face, black ya 丫 style hair individually cut strands down back. Possibly depicting immortal, Lan Caihe 藍菜和. Simple red lapelled tunic over flowered black trousers. Holds fly whisk like object in one hand, small flower basket at crook of elbow. Stilts attached to lower legs, hinged.
