992
Jing head. Wu jing. General or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with black tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large black pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red, yellow, black and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with yellow cut ‘shou’ 壽 on blue ground, black edge. Black and grey painted face, white between brow and eye; downcast red lips.

Jing head. Wu jing. Demon general or supreme commanding general. Bell-shaped filigree natural helmet with pointed spike adorned with red tassel; diadem, ezi 額子, in natural, red, green with central emblem and bravery symbol, large red pompom in front of profile head of green bird, large red pompom behind it; red, blue and green up pointed ornaments; neck cloth with green cut ‘shou’ 壽 on red ground, black edge. Blue painted face with yellow, green, red, white on forehead, black stylized starburst eye; black edged downcast red lips. Pink scalp.
