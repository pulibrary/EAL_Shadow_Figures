Chou head. Lao chou. Scholar. Wen sheng jin with front emblem, in natural with red and green cut deco flowers, green edge, ¾ view, red tassels hang from ruyi style back. Short red back ribbon at nape. Flesh face, cut wrinkles, white tufted brow and three strand opera style beard and stachio obscures mouth. No lacquer.
