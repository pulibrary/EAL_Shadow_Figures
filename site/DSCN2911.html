Jing head. Lao jing Daoist minister. Green, ochre, black, red shamao hat; 2 large red pompoms. Yellow, blue painted face; 3rd eye on forehead; heavy black eye with white stripe parallel to black brow; white filigree pate and full white opera beard covers mouth; characters on neck 天尊. Could be used in FSYY.
