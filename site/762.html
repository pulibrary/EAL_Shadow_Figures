description
Tree of Rebirth property. Bare brown bark tree, hinged high on trunk, with four textile items hanging from branches. Symmetrical crown formed by branches, knots cut and delineated in black. No rods, but rusting metal pin stuck into diamond shaped knot near top center of tree. Red, yellow, blue, green textiles draped over different branches, too high up to be reached with out climbing the tree or flying.
dimensions
H: 22 ½ x 13 ¼; trunk to hinge 12; textiles 3 ½, 4, 4 1/4.; h to textiles 13.
