1049
Dan head. Hua dan. Two tone pleated and knotted green snood covers upturned hair bundle at back. Black hair, short front fringe; thin yellow twisted extension above ear; dark blue ear ring; flesh face, large pink rouge covers eye and cheek; black brow and eye; straight red lips.
1050
Dan head. Hua dan or chou. Two tone knotted blue snood covers hair bundle at back. Black hair; dark green dot earring; pink flesh face with cut wrinkle and jaw; black brow and eye; cut teeth, pink lips, red tongue.
1051
Dan head. Hua dan. Black hair in three large coils at crown, decos of large red to pink flowers with green leaves and buds; thin yellow twisted extension above ear; dark pink and green ear ring; natural face, large pink rouge covers eye and cheek; black brow and eye; straight red lips.
