1232
Jing head. Wu jing demon. Rebel general? Tall black hat with twin pheasant feathers and twin natural furs extending back. Red ear tufts, brow, pate extension sideburn; blue painted face, small circle cuts, startled round eye with natural color outline; stripes of shaded green, blue, red around face; stylized downcast red rubbery lips. Lacquered neck.
1233
Jing head. Wu jing demon. Rebel general. Diadem, ezi 額子, green twisted cloth ciguye 慈菇葉; 1 large black pompom on large black rounded style hat; pheasant feathers and furs at back. Liu Hai hair bumps as with 1142. Brown painted face with black designs, eye and mouth express horror and surprise; green earring; black tuft and beard. Natural lacquer neck.
1234
Jing head. Wu jing rebel commander. Blue green za jin 紮巾 stiff hat with four verticals, each with small red pompoms at top and middle. Diadem, ezi 額子, with pompom and black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbol. Twin pheasant feathers protrude from red and green settings above ears. Twin natural furs extend back. Red painted face, black and white stripes above eye; closed pink lips. Lacquered neck.
