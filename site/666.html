description
Riderless Horse-drawn Carriage. Black steed, grey white brush cut mane draws canopied carriage. Semi ¾ view of horse and cart; ornate saddle and trimmings in natural, red, green, black; intricate knots, few tassels; black, fringed canopy extends from cart to horse’s mane; red ground; ochre crosshatched pattern below lotus, plum, rose lattice colored windows; fringed canopies over windows and rear; dark green top with red ‘shou’ roundel surrounded by brown cut bats, 3 visible of 5; large, 18 spoke, polka dotted, hinged wheel. 2 rods attached at top of carriage and canopy. Note: Folds at horse tail and carriage; movable horse legs and wheel.
dimensions
H: 12 ½ x 19 ¼; horse 11 x 11; wheel dia 6 ½; rod 14 ½.
