Jing head. Jing demon. Manchu close fitting hat with wide brim in dark grey with white trim and top deco; short red ribbon at back. Black scalp, ear tuft and circlet. Slightly yellow painted face with stylized tufted black brow, eye, and marks. Narrow sideburn into two strand long beard, stachio, and soul patch.
