description
Flower screen and rock property. Red lacquer with maroon carved edge repeat pattern, orange, maroon, and cut flower at splats at base. ¾ view shows curved side supports. Cut blue and white profile flowers and leaves from Tai hu like rock, and other leafy maroon plant. On floor near stand is black, grey scholar’s rock in low, rectangular, four legged blue ceramic planter.
dimensions
H: 11 ½ x 8 ¼; flower face 7 x 4; rock 3 ¼ x 3 ¼.
