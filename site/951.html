type
chou_head
description
Chou head. Lao wu chou. Military. Blue lion dog, shizi gou 獅子狗, profile body, full face, green floppy ears, red brows, open mouth assume perched on stiff black gauze hat; natural, red, green diadem, ezi 額子, with central ornament topped by large red pompom and black bravery arrow; large red pompom near dog; green cut ‘shou’ 壽 on red ground, black edge. Flesh skin, cut wrinkles, white clown patch, triangle black eye; grey marking on nose; white cut stachio, beard and soul patch; cut teeth, tongue, lips.
dimensions
H: 4 ¼ x 3.
