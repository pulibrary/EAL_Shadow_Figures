Jing head. Wu. Jing. Demon. Rebel. Officer. White, blush painted face. Stylized black brow, crescent eye. Heavy black greasepaint stachio covers mouth; black to grey down pointing ear tuft. Natural deco diadem, ezi 額子, with black ciguye 慈菇葉 martial arrow, large red pompom. Red ruyi? deco cut helmet and back cloth with natural stemmed lotus; pheasant feather. Natural neck. Lacquered.
