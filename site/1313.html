Jing head. Jing. Wu. Rebel. Black, red, white, grey painted face. Large stylized black eye; serious downcast closed red lips. Close fitting fan wang mao 反王帽, crown of a rebel king; cut decos in red, natural, black, grey, green. Black ciguye 慈菇葉 bravery symbo; 2 large black pompoms; twin pheasant feathers; twin natural furs down back..
