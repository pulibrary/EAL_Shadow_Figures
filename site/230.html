Jing head. Wu jing. White, black, grey face, black lined painted full beard. Military helmet in pale blue, front emblem surrounded by red, blue pompoms, small crown with blue and many red pompoms, twin pheasant feathers extend back from green, red ornament, yellow, black red, dangling ornament over twin white back extending furs.
