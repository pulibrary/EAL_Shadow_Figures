description
Tunic style male body. Peach colored tunic and pants, close sleeves, black cuffs, block hands; black waist sash ties front. Seems that half the body is covered by an open natural white cape or tunic and skirt showing green lining, black edging; black bat, natural white embroidered shoes. 3 rods.
dimensions
H: 8 ½ x 5 ½. Neck rod 12 ¼; hand rods 10.
