Jing head. Wu jing commander. 2 large black pompoms, ciguye 慈菇葉, ying zajin 硬紮斤. Painted face, black to grey, stylized eye area, full black 2 strand pointed beard, stachio, soul patch, teeth, lips. Kiss band member. Lacquered.

Jing head. Wu. Jing. Commander. 2 large red pompoms, ciguye 慈菇葉, ying zajin 硬紮斤. Blue to white painted face, stylized eye area, tufted red sideburn to stachios, soul patch, red lips. Lacquered.

Sheng head. Wu. Sheng. General. Ying zajin 硬紮斤, three rows small white pompoms, black ciguye 慈菇葉, 5 small white pompom decos on it; helmet colors: blue, yellow, black, green, red. White facew with blush, 3 strand grey opera style beard. Unlacquered.
