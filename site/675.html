type
figure
description
Large Gong Musician. Chou profile grey flesh face Manchu pedestrian holds large gong in one hand and beater in other. Cut eye; red Manchu porter’s hat with top knot and black brim, queue partially visible; red wide sleeve, stiff green collar, tunic over black close sleeve tunic; green cuffs and pants tuck into black boots. 2 rods. Note: Gong handle hinged to hand allows free-swinging motion.
dimensions
H: 11 ¼ x 5 ½; gong dia 4 ¼; neck rod 14; rod 12 ½.
