1634
Sheng head. Wu Sheng. Conical bee hive helmet. Coiled layers in red, yellow, blue. Large red pompom over front emblem and bravery symbol. Ochre ribbon ties above ear. Open cut black face, small ochre ear ring. Black hair tucked under hat. 回兵. written on neck in black ink.
1635
Sheng head. Wu Sheng. Conical bee hive coiled hair. Coiled layers of black hair with large top knot; same shape, red cloth ties at front near black bravery symbol. Open cut black face, small same ear ring. Black hair tucked under hat. -?-? 回褰 written on neck in black ink.
