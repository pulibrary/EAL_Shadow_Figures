1687
Jing head. Jing. Demon. Ying shamao 硬紗帽. Third eye on forehead. Prime minister or high ranking civil official. Yellow painted face, white stripe contrasts with heavy black eye. Black stachio, soul patch. Square yellow hat with profile green dragons. Sets of tiny twin blue pompoms at ends of dragon whiskers. Decos in black, red, green, natural. Red ribbons from top to below chin, rosette deco at cheek. 2 large red pompoms.
1688
Jing head. Jing demon. Brown painted face, third eye on forehead. Black stachio, soul patch, pointed beard. Cut deco crown with paired red ribbons on either side. One large each red, black pompom. Cut decos in yellow, red, black.
